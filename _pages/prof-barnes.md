    <p><strong>Dr. Elizabeth (Libby) Barnes</strong> is the Dalton Family Chair in Environmental Data Science &amp; Sustainability, Professor of Computing &amp; Data Sciences, and Professor of Earth &amp; Environment at Boston University, which she joined in 2025.</p>
    <p>Her research focuses on understanding Earth system variability, predictability, and change across time and space — with an emphasis on developing AI tools that maintain scientific interpretability for climate forecasting and human–Earth system interactions.</p>
