We are the **Barnes Research Group**, led by [Prof. Elizabeth (Libby) Barnes](/prof-barnes/) at Boston University. We study Earth system variability, predictability, and change across time and space, developing **explainable and interpretable AI** tools that keep the science transparent.

Our research covers:

- **explainable & interpretable AI** for scientific discovery
- **daily-to-decadal** environmental predictability & prediction
- impacts of **solar climate intervention**
- **human-coupled** Earth system futures

Take a look at our [research](/research/), meet the [group](/group/), watch our [whiteboard animations](/scicomm/), and browse our [publications](/publications/). The latest group [news](/news/) is below.
