## Explainable AI

These videos all start and end the same, highlighting different research activities in the middle.

- **Subseasonal-to-decadal prediction** — climate prediction using explainable AI.
- **Robust & trustworthy AI for climate science** — how explainable AI helps scientists evaluate model trustworthiness.
- **Climate responses & intervention** — detecting Earth system responses to emissions.
- **Forced change detection** — identifying regional change patterns using explainable AI.

## Climate Intervention

Created by the Barnes, Hurrell, and Keys research groups at Colorado State University, with collaborators. Animated by Heartwood Visuals.

- **Introduction to Climate Intervention** — what is climate intervention?
- **Stratospheric Aerosol Injection Scenarios** — simulation scenarios and design choices.
- **Collective Actor Scenario Impacts** — potential impacts of hypothetical global-scale stratospheric aerosol injection.
- **Detection & Perception** — climate variability challenges for intervention detection.
