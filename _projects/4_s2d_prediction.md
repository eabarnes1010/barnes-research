The range from days to decades is where prediction is hardest: too far out for weather models to hold skill, too short for the forced climate signal to rise above the noise. We look for forecasts of opportunity — states where skill is temporarily high — and build the tools, including fast AI emulators, to find and use them.

In practice:

- Predictable modes — MJO, ENSO, PDO — and the windows of opportunity they open
- AI emulators as fast, flexible prediction engines, with honest accounting of where they hold and where they don't
- Identifying not just when a skillful forecast is possible, but why
