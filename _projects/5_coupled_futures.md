The Earth system and human decisions shape each other. We study possible futures of that coupled system — including deliberate interventions like solar climate intervention — and build forecasting tools that account for the costs of the decisions riding on them. A forecast that ignores how it will be used is only half the tool.

In practice:

- Counterfactual and scenario exploration with large emulator ensembles ("what-if" climates)
- Cost-sensitive, reward-aware prediction: tailoring forecasts to the asymmetric risks they inform
- Impacts and trajectories of intentional interventions in the Earth system
